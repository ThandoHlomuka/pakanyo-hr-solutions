About Pakanyo HR Solutions

Pakanyo HR Solutions is a proudly South African HR consulting and skills development company committed to helping businesses build capable, confident, and high-performing teams.

We provide tailored recruitment, leadership development, HR consulting, and accredited training solutions designed to support organisational growth, compliance, and workforce transformation.

Our practical and people-centred approach ensures that businesses receive customised support aligned to their unique operational and workforce needs.

We partner with organisations across multiple industries to develop leaders, strengthen workplace culture, improve productivity, and empower employees through meaningful learning experiences.

Our Story

How We Started

Pakanyo HR Solutions was established to help organisations address workforce capability challenges through practical, compliant, and performance-driven HR and skills development solutions.


We understand the South African learning and development environment and the importance of delivering solutions that align with QCTO, SETA, and SAQA requirements while still creating measurable workplace impact. Our team brings deep expertise across HR, skills development, recruitment, and learning design to deliver integrated solutions that make a real difference.
